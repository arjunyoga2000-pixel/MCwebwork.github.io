FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website? +

A standard business website takes 7–14 working days. An e-commerce website typically takes 15–25 days. We always share a clear timeline before starting.

What is included in the free hosting? +

The first year of domain registration, web hosting and SSL certificate are included free. After 1 year, hosting renewal typically costs ₹2,000–₹4,000 per year.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website? +

No technical knowledge needed. We provide a simple admin panel and train you on how to update content, images and products. You also get 1 month of free support.

Can you redesign my existing website? +

Yes! We offer website redesign and migration services. We can modernise your existing site while keeping your domain, SEO rankings and content intact.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept UPI, bank transfer, Razorpay and cash. We typically work on a 50% advance + 50% on delivery basis for new projects.

Will my website work on mobile phones? +

Absolutely. Every website we build is mobile-first and thoroughly tested on Android and iOS devices across different screen sizes before delivery.
